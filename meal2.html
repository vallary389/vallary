Pasta Alfredo

Ingredients

  • 200g fettuccine pasta
  • 1 cup heavy cream
  • 2 tbsp butter
  • 1/2 cup grated parmesan cheese
  • Garlic, salt, pepper

Instructions

  1. Cook the pasta until al dente.
  2. Melt butter in a pan, add garlic and cream.
  3. Stir in parmesan and let it thicken.
  4. Mix in the pasta and coat well with the sauce.
  5. Garnish with parsley and serve warm!

Nutritional Info (Approx.)

  • Calories: 600 per serving
  • Protein: 18g
  • Fat: 25g
