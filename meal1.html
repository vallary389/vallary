Chicken Biryani

Ingredients

  • 2 cups basmati rice
  • 500g chicken
  • 1 onion (sliced)
  • 2 tomatoes
  • Yogurt, garlic, ginger, spices

Instructions

  1. Marinate chicken with yogurt, garlic, and spices.
  2. Cook onions and tomatoes in a pot.
  3. Add chicken and let it cook until tender.
  4. Boil rice separately, then layer over chicken.
  5. Steam for 10 minutes. Serve hot!

Nutritional Info (Approx.)

  • Calories: 500 per serving
  • Protein: 25g
  • Fat: 15g
